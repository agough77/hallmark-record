## Common Tasks

### Task: Record a Presentation with Webcam

1. Select your **webcam** and **screen**
2. Select your **microphone**
3. Click **Start Recording**
4. Give your presentation
5. Click **Stop Recording**
6. In editor, use **Step 2: Apply Overlay** to add webcam to corner
7. Export!

### Task: Combine Multiple Camera Angles

1. Record from multiple cameras simultaneously
2. In **Step 1**, select the videos and click **Merge**
3. Choose layout (side-by-side or grid)
4. Continue through wizard
5. Export!

### Task: Add Company Branding

1. Complete your recording
2. In **Step 3**, enable watermark
3. Browse to your company logo (PNG with transparency works best)
4. Choose "Top Right" position
5. Set opacity to 70%
6. Apply and export!

## Troubleshooting

### "No devices found"
- Click **ğŸ”„ Refresh Devices**
- Make sure cameras/mics are plugged in
- Check Device Manager (Windows)

### "Recording failed"
- Close other programs using your devices (Zoom, Teams, etc.)
- Try one device at a time to isolate the problem
- Check antivirus isn't blocking the app

### "Export failed"
- Check you have enough disk space
- Make sure output folder exists and is writable
- Try a different quality setting

### "FFmpeg error"
- Reinstall the application
- Check antivirus exceptions
- Contact support if issue persists
